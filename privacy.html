Privacy Policy



EasyMyTrip.co.uk is committed to protecting the privacy of our customers and users. This Privacy Policy outlines the type of information we collect, how we use and protect that information, and your rights as a user of our website.


Information We Collect:

We collect personal information such as name, address, email address, phone number, and payment information when you make a booking with us. We also collect non-personal information such as IP addresses and browser types.

When you visit our website, we may also collect information about your browsing behavior, such as the pages you visit and the links you click. This information is collected through the use of cookies and other tracking technologies.


How We Use Your Information:

We use your personal information to process your bookings and to communicate with you about your travel plans. We may also use your information to send you promotional offers and updates about our services.

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except as necessary to process your bookings or as required by law.

We may use non-personal information for statistical purposes or to improve our website and services. We may also use cookies and other tracking technologies to personalize your experience on our website and to serve targeted advertising.


How We Protect Your Information:

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access or disclosure. We use secure servers and encryption technologies to protect your data.

However, we cannot guarantee the security of your information transmitted through the internet. You acknowledge and accept the risks of transmitting your personal information online.


Your Rights:

You have the right to access and correct your personal information. You may also request that we delete your personal information from our systems.

If you wish to exercise your rights, please contact us at info@easymytrips.co.uk


Marketing Communications:

We may use your personal information to send you marketing communications about our products and services. You can opt out of these communications at any time by following the unsubscribe instructions in the communication or by contacting us at info@easymytrips.co.uk


Cookies and Other Tracking Technologies:

We use cookies and other tracking technologies to personalize your experience on our website and to serve targeted advertising. A cookie is a small text file that is stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit our website. It allows us to recognize your browser and remember your preferences and past actions. We use both session cookies, which expire when you close your browser, and persistent cookies, which remain on your computer or mobile device until they expire or are deleted.

We also use third-party cookies and other tracking technologies, such as Google Analytics and Facebook Pixel, to collect information about your browsing behavior on our website and to serve targeted advertising. You can control cookies and other tracking technologies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may limit your ability to use certain features of our website.
